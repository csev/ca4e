About CMOS Circuit Editor

A web-based CMOS circuit editor for designing and simulating digital logic circuits.

The simulation of the circuut has limitations. It can design a two-transistor Not gate but falls short of a full featured logic simulator when building more complex circuits like NAND, NOR, XOR, etc.

Features:

  • NMOS and PMOS transistors
  • Switches and probes
  • Real-time simulation
  • Component labeling
  • Interactive wire connections
