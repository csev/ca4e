
Accessibility Audit
-------------------

This summarizes the results of accessibiltiy work wiht the CA4E slide decks.

First I went through in PowerPoint and fixed every issue identified in PowerPoint
except when it the "kind of dumb" slide reading order analysis would have caused
bad understandability for either the visual versions or accessible versions. A simple
example is a figure that goes in a circle repeatedly.  The audit tools want top to
bottom reading or left to right reading.  A circlular reading pattern cannot do either.
Similarly is there are two columns and within each of the two columns there is three
columns, it needs to be read in a way that semantically links the concepts - not pure
left-to-right top to bottom.

I note below where there are reading order issues that cannot be fixed without
breaking understanding.

After things were as clean as possible in PPT - I exported them to PDF and then
checked both the PPT and PDF's with notes below.  I would just mark all the
reading order stuff in Panorama as "checked".
